知識產權管理建議

IP Management Recommendations

0/10 完成

點擊每個步驟了解更多詳情 | Click on each step to learn more details

1

計劃 Plan

根據需要，製訂如何獲取、保護和使用知識產權資產的具體策略。

Based on your needs, plan clearly on how to get, protect, and use intellectual property (IP) assets.

2

盤點 Inventory

清楚你有哪些知識產權資產，以及它們的性質及潛在價值。

Identify the IP assets you own, understand their nature, and assess their potential value.

3

記錄 Record

把知識產權資產的資料整理及清楚記下，並及時註冊保護。

Organize and document all IP information and register them promptly for protection.

4

投資 Invest

投資足夠的金錢和人手資源去管理及維護知識產權。

Allocate sufficient money and manpower to manage and maintain your IP.

5

檢視 Monitor

定期檢視知識產權的狀態，以評估潛在問題及風險。

Regularly review the status of your IP to identify potential issues and risks.

6

跟隨 Follow

遵守知識產權的規矩，保護好知識產權資產。

Strictly follow the IP regulations to protect your IP assets.

7

防護 Safeguard

警惕任何對你的知識產權資產的侵權行為或未經授權的使用，發現就馬上處理。

Watch for any infringement or unauthorised use of your IP and deal with it immediately.

8

保障 Protect

適當時須通過談判或法律訴訟保護知識產權，免受侵犯。

When necessary, protect your IP through negotiation or lawsuits.

9

利潤 Profit

商業化知識產權資產，比如授權、合夥等商業安排。

Commercialise your IP through licensing, partnerships, or other business arrangements.

10

驗證 Verify

交易前，仔細調查其知識產權有沒有問題，確保安全。

Before any transaction, carefully verify the IP to ensure it is free of risks.
